Our Services

01

Company Formation (Mainland, Freezone, Offshore)​

Start your business journey. We handle the entire process of company registration, whether you’re establishing a mainland, freezone, or offshore entity.

02

Golden Visa Services​

Unlock exclusive residency benefits with our Golden Visa services. We guide you through the eligibility criteria and application process to secure long-term residency in the UAE.

03

PRO Services​

Leave the paperwork to us! Our PRO services include document attestation, government approvals, and other administrative tasks, ensuring you meet all regulatory requirements.

Ejari Services

Ensure compliance with Dubai rental laws. We provide fast and accurate Ejari registration services, helping you get your tenancy contracts officially registered.

Visa Process (A-Z)​

From application to stamping, we handle every step of the visa process for employees, investors, and families. Trust us to make your visa journey hassle-free.

Trade License Renewal Services​
